What is an Extension?

Extensions add new capabilities to the Gemini CLI, allowing you to customize your workflow and integrate with other tools.

What is a Resource?

Resources are external references, documentation, and guides that help you get the most out of Gemini CLI.

What is an MCP?

Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers provide context and tools to AI models, enabling them to interact with your local environment and data.
